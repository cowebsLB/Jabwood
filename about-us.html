OUR JOURNEY

Growing Like the Trees

Just like the trees from which our wood is produced, the Jabado family matured to become a highly acclaimed company

1930s

The Beginning

Mr. Mahmoud Jabado established a small shop of retail wood planks near Beirut harbor with a yearly sale of 4,000 CBM. A humble beginning that would grow into a legacy.

1950s-1960s

Family Growth

The family business expanded, with multiple generations joining to build upon the foundation of quality and trust established by Mr. Mahmoud Jabado.

1970s-1980s

Regional Expansion

From a traditional family lumberyard with customers within a 10 km radius, Jabwood transformed into a multi-division company, expanding throughout Lebanon and beyond.

1990s-2000s

Middle East Leader

Jabwood evolved into one of the leading timber companies in the Arab World, supplying the Middle East market with premium wood products across Lebanon, Jordan, and Saudi Arabia.

Today

Industry Excellence

Now operating at 400,000 CBM annually, Jabwood is recognized among the top timber trading companies worldwide, maintaining the same traditional family values that built our foundation.

Jabado Family

Generations of Excellence

Our Story

The amazing story of the Jabado family started a long time ago, back in the 1930's, in a small shop of retail wood planks with a yearly sale of 4,000 CBM owned by Mr. Mahmoud Jabado near Beirut harbor.

Just like the trees from which their wood is produced, the family matured to become a highly acclaimed company that both imports and exports the finest wood.

Because for close to half a century we've been a growing concern, evolving from a traditional family lumberyard to a multi-division company supplying the Middle East with premium timber products.
