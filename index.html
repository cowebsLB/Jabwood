About Jabwood

For over 50 years, Jabwood has been a trusted name in the wood industry, providing premium quality hard woods, soft woods, and wood-based panels to customers throughout Lebanon and beyond.

Based in Tripoli, Lebanon, we pride ourselves on our commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction. Our extensive product range includes everything from exotic hardwoods to engineered wood panels.
