# Jabwood Wood Products Website

A bilingual (Arabic/English) website for Jabwood - a wood products company. Features product catalog, company information, branch locations, and comprehensive product listings for hardwoods, softwoods, and wood-based panels.

## Technology Stack

HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, i18n

## Features

- **Bilingual Support**:
  - Full Arabic and English support
  - Language switching
  - i18n implementation
- **Product Catalog**:
  - Hardwoods (Cherry, Iroko, Maple, Ramin, Red Oak, Sapeli, Teak, Walnut, Wawa, etc.)
  - Softwoods (Cedar, Redwood, Douglas Fir, Whitewood)
  - Wood-based panels (BlockBoard, Hardboards, Melaminated Boards, Plywood, MDF, Chipboard)
- **Company Information**:
  - About us page
  - Company history
  - Factory information
  - Chairman information
- **Branch Locations**:
  - Branch information
  - Contact details
- **Product Pages**:
  - Individual product pages
  - Product images
  - Product descriptions
  - Category organization

## Project Structure

```
- `index.html` - Homepage
- `about-us.html` - About page
- `branches.html` - Branch locations
- `contact.html` - Contact page
- `our-products.html` - Products listing
- `products/` - Product category pages
  - `hard-woods/` - Hardwood products
  - `soft-woods/` - Softwood products
  - `wood-based-panels/` - Panel products
- `jabwood old website/` - Legacy website version
- `js/` - JavaScript files
  - `main.js` - Main functionality
  - `i18n.js` - Internationalization
  - `languages/` - Translation files
    - `ar.json` - Arabic translations
    - `en.json` - English translations
- `css/custom.css` - Custom styles
- `assets/` - Static assets
  - `images/` - Product and company images
  - `icons/` - Favicon and app icons
```

# Jabwood

Premium Wood Products from Tripoli, Lebanon

## About

Jabwood has been providing premium quality wood products for over 50 years. Founded in 1974 in Tripoli, Lebanon, we specialize in:

- **Hard Woods**: Oak, Teak, Walnut, Cherry, Maple, and more
- **Soft Woods**: Cedar, Redwood, Douglas Fir, Whitewood
- **Wood-Based Panels**: Plywood, MDF, Chipboard, Blockboard, and engineered solutions

## Our Legacy

- **1974**: Founded in Tripoli, Lebanon
- **1985**: Expanded operations and product range
- **1995**: Introduced engineered wood solutions
- **2010**: Modernized facilities with latest technology
- **Today**: 50+ years of excellence in wood products
