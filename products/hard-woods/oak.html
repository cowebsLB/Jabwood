About Oak Wood

Oak is one of the most popular and versatile hardwoods available. Known for its exceptional strength and durability, oak wood is perfect for a wide range of applications including furniture, flooring, cabinetry, and construction.

Our oak wood is carefully selected and processed to ensure the highest quality standards. Each piece is inspected for grain consistency, color uniformity, and structural integrity.

Key Features:

  • Exceptional strength and durability
  • Beautiful natural grain patterns
  • Excellent workability
  • Resistant to wear and tear
  • Stains and finishes well
  • Long-lasting and sustainable

Common Applications

Furniture

  • • Dining tables and chairs
  • • Bedroom furniture
  • • Office desks
  • • Cabinets and storage

Construction

  • • Flooring and decking
  • • Structural beams
  • • Doors and windows
  • • Trim and molding

Specifications

Type: Hardwood
Density: High
Color: Light to medium brown
Grain: Straight
Hardness: Very Hard
