About Cedar Wood

Cedar is a beautiful softwood prized for its distinctive reddish-brown color, straight grain, and pleasant natural aroma. It's naturally resistant to insects and decay, making it perfect for both indoor and outdoor applications.

Our cedar wood is carefully selected for its quality and consistency. The wood's natural oils provide protection against moisture and insects, while its fine grain makes it easy to work with hand or power tools.

Key Features:

  • Distinctive reddish-brown color
  • Pleasant natural aroma
  • Naturally insect and decay resistant
  • Excellent workability
  • Lightweight yet durable
  • Stable and dimensionally sound

Common Applications

Indoor Uses

  • • Closets and storage chests
  • • Interior paneling
  • • Furniture and cabinetry
  • • Decorative trim

Outdoor Uses

  • • Fencing and gates
  • • Decking and siding
  • • Outdoor furniture
  • • Garden structures

Specifications

Type: Softwood

Color: Reddish-brown
Grain: Straight
